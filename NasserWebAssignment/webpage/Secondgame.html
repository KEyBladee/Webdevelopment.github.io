Second Game

Final fantasy 14 is one of my favourite games that I have played. I have alot of playtime in it and have finished everything in the game that I care about, With over 20,000 hours played.

Pros

  • Best MMO graphics todate.
  • Polished gameplay and animations
  • Best Community interactions I have had.

Cons

  • Input lag that is very noticable in highend content
  • It has all of your attention when you are in the zone with it
  • Very sensitive chat with strict rules

I have gave it multiple chance but I do not like it in todays modern standards but will play it if friends are.
